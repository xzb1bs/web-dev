Recipe #2

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
kefir 250 ml
eggs 3
sugar 1 cup
flour 160 g
apples 650 g

Step 1:Whisk kefir with eggs and vegetable oil until smooth.

Step 2:Add sugar and vanilla sugar. Whisk again with a whisk. Sift the flour together with baking powder and salt into the dough. Mix the dough well, just stir it with a fork. As you can see, the dough turns out to be quite liquid. It should resemble pancake batter or thin sour cream. If you have not added all the flour, then at this stage, if necessary, add a little more flour.

Step 3:Add the sliced apples to the batter. Mix the dough well with the apples and let it stand for 10 minutes so that each apple slice is soaked in the dough.

Step 4:Bake the pie in a preheated 180-190 degree oven for about 35-40 minutes.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
meet 800 gr
rice 2 cups
carrot 2
water 1 cup

Step 1:Send the meat to roast. Cook, stirring, until golden brown.

Step 2:Add the onion, carrot and garlic to the cauldron. Fry for a couple of minutes until soft. If necessary, add a little more vegetable oil.

Step 3:Rinse the rice several times until it reaches clean water. Pour it into the cauldron and smooth it out with a spatula or spoon. Dip a whole head of garlic into the rice. If necessary, add a little more water. Make sure that the rice is completely immersed in water.

Step 4:Leave to cook over medium heat for 20-30 minutes.
