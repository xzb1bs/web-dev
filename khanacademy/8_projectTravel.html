Today we will tell you and show you the most beautiful and interesting places in Kazakhstan!

  • Medeu

    The Medeu is an outdoor speed skating and bandy rink. It is located in a mountain valley on the south-eastern outskirts of Almaty, Kazakhstan. Medeu sits 1,691 metres above sea level. It consists of 10.5 thousand square meters of ice and utilizes a sophisticated freezing and watering system to ensure the quality of the ice.

  • Kolsay Lakes

    The Kolsai Lakes are a chain of three turquoise mountain lakes on the Kolsai River in the mountains of the Northern Tien Shan. The lakes are located on the territory of the Kegensky district of the Almaty region of Kazakhstan and are part of the Kolsai Lakes State National Nature Park. In addition to them, the park includes Lake Kaindy, well-known for the picturesque trunks of dry fir trees sticking out of the water, and other natural places interesting for recreation and tourism.

  • Charyn Canyon

    Charyn Canyon is a canyon on the Sharyn River in Kazakhstan (200 kilometres (120 mi) east of Almaty, close to the Chinese border). The canyon is roughly 154 kilometres in length. It is part of the Charyn National Park, and is located within the territory of the Uygur District, Raiymbek District and Enbekshikazakh District in the Almaty Region.
